Business

Business In Naawi-Oodena

Naawi-Oodena features a growing business area that supports Indigenous entrepreneurship and local economic development. The community includes shops, restaurants, and service-based businesses that provide jobs and essential services while reflecting Indigenous culture and values. These businesses help build a self-sustaining economy and offer a welcoming space for both residents and visitors.

Education in Naawi-Oodena

Naawi-Oodena supports education through culturally grounded programs and schools that reflect Indigenous knowledge, languages, and traditions. The community emphasizes lifelong learning by offering resources for students of all ages, including early childhood education, primary and secondary schooling, and adult learning opportunities.

Naawi-Oodena is home to cultural campuses that preserve and share Indigenous knowledge, language, and traditions. These spaces offer programs in history, art, storytelling, and traditional practices. They host events and ceremonies, bringing people together to learn from Elders and Knowledge Keepers.

What is Naawi-Oodena?

Naawi-Oodena, meaning "centre of the heart and community" in Anishinaabemowin, is Canada's largest urban reserve, located in southwest Winnipeg. Spanning approximately 160 acres, it is jointly managed by all seven Treaty 1 First Nations and the Canada Lands Company. Formerly the Kapyong Barracks, this site has been transformed into a thriving community that blends Indigenous culture with modern urban living. Naawi-Oodenan aims to generate economic investments and connect communities together.

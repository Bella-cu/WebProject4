Buisness In Naawi-Oodena

Naawi-Oodena features a growing business area that supports Indigenous entrepreneurship and local economic development. The community includes shops, restaurants, and service-based businesses that provide jobs and essential services while reflecting Indigenous culture and values. These businesses help build a self-sustaining economy and offer a welcoming space for both residents and visitors.

Education in Naawi-Oodena

Naawi-Oodena supports education through culturally grounded programs and schools that reflect Indigenous knowledge, languages, and traditions. The community emphasizes lifelong learning by offering resources for students of all ages, including early childhood education, primary and secondary schooling, and adult learning opportunities. Education in Naawi-Oodena is designed to empower youth, strengthen identity, and prepare future leaders.

Naawi-Oodena is home to cultural campuses that are dedicated to preserving and sharing Indigenous knowledge, language, and traditions. These campuses serve as important educational and community hubs, offering programs in history, art, storytelling, and traditional practices. They are designed to reflect the values of the Nations involved in the Treaty One area, creating a space where culture is not only remembered but actively lived and taught. The campuses also host events, workshops, and ceremonies that bring people together to learn from Elders and Knowledge Keepers. By combining modern education with cultural teachings, these spaces help strengthen identity and pride among youth while fostering understanding and respect across generations.

Naawi-Oodena offers a variety of places that meet the community’s everyday needs and bring people together. Local shops provide handmade goods, cultural items, and everyday essentials, while restaurants and cafés serve both traditional Indigenous meals and modern dishes, creating a welcoming space to enjoy good food and connect. The community also includes schools, a public library with books, computers, and quiet study areas, along with health centers, daycare, and community centers. Together, these services make Naawi-Oodena a well-rounded and inviting place to live, learn, work, and gather.
